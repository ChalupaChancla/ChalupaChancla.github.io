What is Animal Crossing?

Animal Crossing is an online game you can play with friends. It's a social simulation game. You can personalize your town and make friends. Bells is the type of currency in Animal Crossing. In order to make bells you can go fishing, catch bugs, and sell some items at Nook Cranny's shop!

5 Facts About Animal Crossing

  1. It cost 60 dollars in the US.
  2. You can have up to 10 villagers in a town.
  3. Tom Nook is the owner and founder of Nook Inc.
  4. You start with 2 villagers and keep getting more until u reach 10 villagers.
  5. You can only play Animal Crossing on a nintendon switch.
